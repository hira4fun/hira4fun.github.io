Drawn to Reality

  • Zoe Johnson |
  • Projects

One Week Game Jam with Friends, where I acted as Lead Designer.

Drawn to Reality is a short platformer game where you play as Seidan, a scaredy cat who is trying to overcome his fears with his best friend Fuki. Enter Seidan's Dream Journal and help him to overcome his fears in a short but sweet platformer with 6 levels. This game was my first game jam, and many of my friends first time using unity, and I am pretty satisfied with how it turned out, even if it still has a few quirks/bugs.

My primary role in this project was conceptualizing and designing the mechanics and helping to create the story, and designing/implementing the UI/UX elements such as the menus and dialogue system. I also helped to create the music in the game, creating midi versions of a song composed by one of my friends to use for the levels, assisted in creating art assets, and coded several of the systems and mechanics in the game such as the entire overworld screen and the dialogue.
