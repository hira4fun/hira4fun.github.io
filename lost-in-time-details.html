Lost in Time

  • Zoe Johnson |

Capstone Project Done with One other Person

Lost in Time is a Metroidvania-inspired dungeon crawler, where you explore one big dungeon to unlock its secrets and find a way out. The player will unlock more characters to play as as they traverse through the dungeon, each of which having unique playstyles and abilities that can solve different puzzles to progress further into the dungeon. I worked on this project with one other person, who focused on the combat system. Although the scope was larger, it had to be scoped down a bit as my partner was less familiar with Unity so I ended up picking up a bit of extra workload and had to limit the scope a bit as a result.

My responsibilities for this game included conceptualizing and designing mechanics and puzzle design, as well as acting as the lead programmer and designed all puzzle mechanics, designed art and music assets, and handled all UI/UX elements and HUD elements, the main things I did not do for this project was the combat system.
