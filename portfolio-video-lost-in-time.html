Lost in Time

Lost in Time is a Metroidvania-inspired dungeon crawler, where you explore one big dungeon to unlock its secrets and find a way out. The player will unlock more characters to play as as they traverse through the dungeon, each of which having unique playstyles and abilities that can solve different puzzles to progress further into the dungeon.

Conceptualized and designed mechanics for a dungeon crawling puzzle game made by me and a partner for a capstone project. Acted as lead programmer and designed all puzzle mechanics, designed art and music assets, and handled all UI/UX elements and HUD elements.
