Short Twine Game Text Adventure

Night and Nyagic is an interactive text adventure game created in twine. Play as a silly little familiar cat as they look after their owner's potion shop while she is gone as you get into silly shenangians and hijinks.

This game was entirely made and coded by me in twine. I used sugarcube in order to have access to javascript when formatting it, allowing for a lot of neat systems like the day/night cycle and energy system.
